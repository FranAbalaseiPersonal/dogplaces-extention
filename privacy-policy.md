DogPlaces Extension – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Overview

DogPlaces is a Chrome extension that helps users quickly see whether a restaurant or café is dog-friendly when browsing Google Maps.
This extension is designed with privacy in mind and does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data from users.

We believe in complete transparency, so this document explains what data we do (and do not) access.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

DogPlaces does not collect any of the following:

Personally identifiable information

Email, name, address, phone number

Financial, authentication, or health information

Browser history

Keystrokes

Cookies

Device identifiers

IP address

Location data from your device

Any content from other websites

We have no servers storing user-related data.

3. What the Extension Does Access

To function, the DogPlaces extension:

Reads the current Google Maps URL only to extract the restaurant name.

Fetches public dog-friendly information from our Supabase database.

No personal data about the user is processed.

The extension never:

Tracks which restaurants you search

Monitors your browsing

Logs your behaviour

Sends analytics

4. Third-Party Services

The extension connects to our database hosted on Supabase to retrieve dog-friendly status for locations.
No personal or device data is ever sent to Supabase.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We do not use:

Cookies

Tracking technologies

Analytics

Advertising networks

The extension has zero tracking.

6. Changes to This Policy

If future versions introduce optional accounts, user submissions, or analytics, this policy will be updated and re-published.
Users will always be able to review the latest version.
